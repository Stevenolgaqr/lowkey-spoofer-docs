### Step 3: Extract and Prepare

### Step 5: Save Your Serials

**CRITICAL STEP!**

1. Go to **"Device Info"** tab
2. **Screenshot** ALL your serials with Snipping Tool

### Step 6: Permanent Spoof

1. Go to **"Spoof"** tab
2. Click **"Permanent Spoof"**
3. Your motherboard should be **automatically detected**
4. Click **"SPOOF"**

**If NOT detected:**
- Click on **"Other"** button
- Select your motherboard manually
- Then click spoof

---

### Step 7: MAC Address Spoofing

**There are 2 options:**

#### Option A: Ethernet (Cable) Users
**Use:** **PERM MAC SPOOF** 
- For Intel and Realtek adapters
- Permanent spoof that persists

#### Option B: WiFi Users
**Use:** **WIFI MAC SPOOF**
- For WiFi adapters
- Also works for Ethernet users if PERM doesn't work

**Steps:**
1. Select appropriate MAC spoof option
2. Click **Spoof MAC**
3. Wait for confirmation

---

### Step 8: Restart PC

1. **Restart** your computer
2. **Wait** for Windows to fully load

---

1. Open Lowkey Loader again
